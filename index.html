Our Vision
Community • Resilience • Legacy
UnityVerse is more than a soccer portal — it’s a space where we build brotherhood, black resiliency, autonomy, and responsibility. We want every member to feel part of something bigger than a game: a community that respects each other, supports each other, and shows up.

We also know our kids are watching us. Through UnityVerse, we want to model:
  • Respect on and off the field
  • Commitment and reliability
  • Healthy competition with love and fun
  • Support for each other’s families and journeys
What We Expect From Each Member
Values • Behaviour
  • • Show up when you say you will — your word matters.
  • • Respect everyone: teammates, opponents, referees, and yourself.
  • • Play hard, but never forget fun and safety.
  • • Communicate clearly in the group — no ghosting.
  • • Be a model for the younger ones watching us.
  • • Protect the spirit of the group: no drama, no disrespect.
